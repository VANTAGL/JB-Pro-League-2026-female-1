# JB Pro League 2026 Female #1

![Dates](https://img.shields.io/badge/Date-Jan%2017%20%E2%80%93%20Feb%2015%2C%202026-blue) ![Prize Pool](https://img.shields.io/badge/Prize%20Pool-$25,000-brightgreen)

**JB.com**, together with **@vantagl**, are proud to officially announce **JB Pro League 2026 Female #1** — a Tier 2 Counter-Strike 2 online tournament with a **$25,000** prize pool, taking place between **January 17 - February 15, 2026**. with Female Rosters based in Europe

---

## Tournament Format & Schedule

### Stage 1 — Open Qualifiers
- **Max qualifier cap:** 64 teams
- **Matches:** All matches are BO3
- **Dates:** January 17 — February 8, 2026
- **Match times:** 10:00, 13:00, 16:00, 19:00 CET
- **Dates per qualifier:**
  - Qualifier 1: January 17–18, 2026
  - Qualifier 2: January 24–25, 2026
  - Qualifier 3: January 31 — February 1, 2026
  - Qualifier 4: February 7–8, 2026
- **Advancement:** Top 2 teams progress from each qualifier
- **Seeding:** All matches are played out for seeding


### Main Event
- **Matches:** All matches are BO3
- **Group Stage (GSL Groups, 8 teams):** February 9 — 12, 2026 — 2 teams progress from each group
- **Playoffs (Double Elimination, 4 teams):** February 13 — 15, 2026
